Loui Lobo
Louilobo@unm.edu

PROFILE

A mascot for the University of New Mexico. Has been a mascot at the school since 1920. He wasn’t at athletics until around the early 1960s and a human mascot was created. They also created a partner for him named Lucy Lobo in the early 1980’s. Loui Lobo is a part of the spirit squad here at UNM.

PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

Can host events and attend school events. His job is to make sure people and the students are having fun at these school events. Iced has had a lot of experience working with others to make the campus a fun space for everyone. He is very active in his community and helping others around him.
